Our Heritage Story

Where Every Piece Has a Soul

At Rust & Roses, we believe that true beauty lies in the stories behind each piece. Our journey began with a simple mission: to preserve and celebrate the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship while making it accessible to the modern world.

Every item in our collection is handpicked from skilled artisans across India, each carrying generations of knowledge and passion. From the bustling markets of Rajasthan to the weaving villages of Bengal, we partner with craftspeople who pour their hearts into every creation.
